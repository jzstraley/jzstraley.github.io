Welcome Message

 Hi there! Welcome to the CAMC Family. To help ease the transition from 4th year Medical Student to Resident Physician, I've complied my notes from 1st year on this website. I built this website and Intern Guide with the goal to help you become familiar with the ins and outs of being a resident at CAMC. You will find there resident basics, organization tips, and important websites. Please send me feedback for improvement below. I hope this helps!
