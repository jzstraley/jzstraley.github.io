 Hi there! If you're reading this, you're likely in the right place. Welcome to CAMC! To help ease the transition from Medical Student to Resident Physician, I've complied my notes from the past 2 years on this website. I built this website and Intern Guide with the goal to help you become familiar with the ins and outs of being a resident at CAMC. You will find there resident basics, organization tips, and important websites. Please send me feedback for improvement below. I hope this helps!
